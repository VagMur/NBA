Top 3 guards for the 2020 NBA Draft




Anthony Edwards:

Bruising scorer who can create space with his dribble and make tough shots from all over the court, but his decision-making leaves much to be desired.

Comparisons to Donovan Mitchell and Dion Waiters



Lamelo Ball

He needs to dramatically improve his scoring efficiency, but he has the upside to become one of the NBA’s best playmakers.

Comparisons to Jason Williams and Rajon Rondo



Killian Hayes

Dynamic left-handed shot maker who’s made rapid progress at age 18, though he’s raw in some technical areas. He is also a very strong playmaker

Comparisons to D'angelo Russell and Tony Parker
